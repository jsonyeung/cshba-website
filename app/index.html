Keeping Heritage Traditions
in Vancouver Chinatown

Anchoring Changes, Reviving Traditions

Vancouver Chinatown is unique. It is important to the City of Vancouver which would like to see it vigorously maintained and revitalized. Vancouver Chinatown is unique in that its history of close to 120 years has witnessed the growth of the originally ghettorized Chinese community and its gradual integration into mainstream Canadian society.
